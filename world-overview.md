# World Overview

This document contains the core overview of your world. As you develop your world, you'll add information about:

- **Concept**: What makes your world unique and interesting
- **Physical characteristics**: Geography, climate, natural features
- **Major regions**: Distinctive areas and their characteristics
- **Inhabitants**: Beings that populate the world
- **Systems**: How magic, technology, or other systems work
- **History**: Major events and historical periods

Start by describing your world's core concept and build from there.
